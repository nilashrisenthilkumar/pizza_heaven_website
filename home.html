ABOUT PIZZA HEAVEN

Legacy of Pizza Heaven

Pizza Heaven is more than just a pizza shop; it is a beacon of culinary excellence, sustainability, and community spirit. Founded in the heart of our bustling city over three decades ago, Pizza Heaven has grown from a small, family-owned establishment into a beloved institution, known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and responsible business practices.

Humble Beginnings

Pizza Heaven was founded in 1990 by Maria and Giovanni Russo, two passionate food lovers with a dream of bringing authentic Italian pizza to their local community. With a single oven and a handful of cherished family recipes, they set out to create a pizzeria that would offer more than just food; they wanted to create an experience that would bring people together. From the very beginning, Maria and Giovanni believed in using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. This commitment to quality quickly set Pizza Heaven apart from other pizza shops in the area.

Commitment to Sustainability

As Pizza Heaven grew, so did its commitment to sustainability. Long before it became a trend, Maria and Giovanni understood the importance of caring for the environment. They implemented eco-friendly practices such as using biodegradable packaging, reducing waste, and sourcing ingredients from local organic farms. In the early 2000s, Pizza Heaven became one of the first pizzerias in the city to achieve a zero-waste certification. This dedication to sustainability extends beyond the kitchen. Pizza Heaven has partnered with local organizations to support environmental initiatives, such as tree planting programs and community clean-up events. Their efforts have not only helped to reduce their carbon footprint but have also inspired other businesses in the community to adopt more sustainable practices.

Awards and Recognition

Over the years, Pizza Heaven has received numerous awards and accolades for their exceptional food, service, and community contributions. They have been featured in local and national media, and their pizzas have been recognized as some of the best in the country. Despite this recognition, the team at Pizza Heaven remains humble and dedicated to their mission of providing great food and making a positive impact.
